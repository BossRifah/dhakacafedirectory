7. **brew-and-bites.jpg** - Brew & Bites Café (Banani)
8. **crimson-cup-coffee.jpg** - Crimson Cup Coffee (Banani)

## Recommended Image Specifications:
- **Format**: JPEG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: Minimum 1200x800px (landscape orientation)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 3:2 or 16:9
- **File Size**: Under 500KB (optimized for web)
- **Quality**: High-quality photos showing the café interior or exterior

## How to Add Images:
1. Save your café photos with the exact filenames listed above
2. Place them in this directory (`public/images/shops/`)
3. The website will automatically display them on both:
   - Homepage card previews (with hover zoom effect)
   - Individual shop pages (as hero images)

## Fallback Behavior:
If images are not added, the website will display colorful gradient backgrounds with coffee emojis instead.
